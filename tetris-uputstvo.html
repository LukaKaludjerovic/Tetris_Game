INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome! Thank you for playing our game!

Firstly, you need choose the level of the game. Blocks will fall slower or faster, depending on the chosen level.

Secondly, you need to select all blocks you want to exist in the game.

Control blocks by using keyboard arrows Left, Down and Right. Left and Right arrow move block to the left or right, respectfully, whereas Down arrow moves block down faster.

Rotate blocks by using keys A and D. Key A rotates block counterclockwise, whereas Key D rotates block clockwise.

When the row is full it will disappear, while your score will be increased, depending on which level are you playing on and with how many blocks.
